About

I'm not an expert. In fact, I'm a bit of a rookie. Just over a year ago, I decided to start learning how to write code. I have always had an interest in technology, as well as ideas that I have wanted to implement, but lacked the knowledge to actually create anything. Prior to this, my knowledge of programming was limited to writing a spreadsheet sum function. After about a year of research and learning on my own, using free resources, I have decided to blog my education and attempts at writing code here, to record my progress as well as for use by anyone who may be on similar path.

I am a big proponent of learning by doing, so I will mostly be working on projects and hopefully picking up the necessary skills along the way. My first goal is to create the blog that you are currently reading. I have decided to create the blog using Mongo DB, Angular, Express, and Node.js, a.k.a. the MEAN stack. Well, that is what I want the finished product to look like anyway, but I want to get it up and running asap, so I'll start with a basic webpage and build it up as I go along.

If you have any advice, questions, concerns, critiques, or anything of that nature feel free to let me know. I'm open to advice, and I'll try to respond to, and thank contributors in a timely manner. Just email me at: theamateur@amateurcode.com, or follow me on Twitter: @AmateurCode.
